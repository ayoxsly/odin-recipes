Peanut Butter

Designed by Freepik

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 Pumpkin
  • Stock
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut pumpkin into slices. Remove seeds.
  2. Place pumpkin, stock and water in a pot. Bring to a boil.
  3. Remove from heat and use stick blender to blend.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste. Stir.
